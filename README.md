# Marketing-Campaign-Performance-Analysis
Marketing Campaign Analysis using Excel (Power Query), SQL and Business Insights.
## Project Overview

Marketing teams collect a large amount of campaign data, but the real value comes from analysing that data to understand campaign performance and support business decisions.

In this project, three separate campaign datasets were combined into a single master dataset using Power Query. After preparing and cleaning the data in Excel, SQL was used to answer business questions related to campaign performance, ROI, conversions, revenue and acquisition cost.

The final output includes business observations and recommendations based on the analysis.

## Business Objective

The objective of this project was to analyse marketing campaign performance and identify patterns that could help improve future campaigns.

The analysis focuses on:

- Campaign Performance
- Revenue
- ROI
- Conversion Rate
- Acquisition Cost
- Customer Targeting
- Campaign Duration
- Brand Comparison

## Dataset

Source: Kaggle {https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sshriya08/multi-brand-marketing-campaign-performance-dataset}

The original dataset consisted of three separate Excel files representing marketing campaigns for:

- Nykaa
- Purplle
- Tira

These files were combined into one master dataset using Microsoft Excel Power Query before performing further analysis.

## Business Questions Solved

The following business questions were explored using SQL:

1. Which campaign type is getting the best response with respect to highest amount of conversions? Does the conversion rate for output says the same ?
2. Which target audience recorded the lowest YouTube clicks in 2024, and despite having the fewest clicks, did it generate a higher conversion rate than the overall average YouTube campaign conversion rate in 2024 ?
3. Considering Instagram and facebook, which platform generated more ROI while targeting the youth. Compare the numbers ?
4. Compare nykaa v/s tiraa v/s pruplle “paid ads campaigns” on the basis of clicks they have got. Did the top 5 justifies with the conversion rates too? Give reason for same.
5. The agency wants to cut its marketing budget by 20% next quarter. Identify the bottom 20% of campaigns by ROI that together account for the highest Acquisition Cost. How much budget would be saved if these campaigns were discontinued, and how much Revenue would be sacrificed ?
6. How does the duration of any campaign relate to its roi? Does longer run campaigns give higher roi and lesser one gives the low roi or vice versa ?
7. Check how much conversions does this kind off campaigns make in compare to all total conversions this data has. Also which brand is getting more ROI with influencer campaigns in compare to the average ROI being generated by influencer campaigns ?
8. How much share of acquisition cost does college students and working women take in compare to the overall cost spent by the agency for all these campaigns. Did the one taking lesser generated more revenue in compare to the other ?
9. From the data, July 2024 - June 2025, has the revenue increased or decreased from the “Email” & “SEO” (calculated seperately) campaigns targeted for the “Youth” ?
10. In this agency’s portfolio, how much % of Revenue was generated by the target audience which overall had the least amount of conversions (and how much revenue, show the sum in answer). 

## Key Findings

- Email campaigns generated the highest number of conversions among all campaign types.
- Nearly 20% of total conversions came from Influencer campaigns.
- Facebook campaigns performed better than Instagram for the Youth audience in terms of ROI.
- Campaign duration showed very little impact on average ROI.
- College Students recorded the lowest conversions but still contributed around 20% of the total revenue.
- Working Women and College Students together accounted for approximately 40% of the total acquisition cost.
- Email and SEO campaign revenue declined towards the end of the analysed time period.
- High-click Paid Ads campaigns did not always result in high conversion rates.

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can be considered:

- Continue investing in Email campaigns due to their strong conversion performance.
- Review acquisition spending for audience groups with lower returns.
- Investigate the decline in Email and SEO revenue during the later months.
- Evaluate campaign quality instead of relying only on click volume.
- Continue monitoring campaign ROI across different audience segments before increasing marketing spend.
